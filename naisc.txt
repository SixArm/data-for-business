# NAICS: North American Industry Classification System 

The NAICS numbering system employs a six-digit number code 
to categories industry levels:.

  * The first two digits designate the business sector.

  * The third digit designates the business subsector.

  * The fourth digit designates the industry group.

  * The fifth digit designates the NAICS industries.

  * The sixth digit designates the national industries.

The first five digits are generally (although not always strictly)
the same in all three countries.
